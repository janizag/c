Janiza G. Corrales

Hi, I'm Janiza (Gesmundo) Corrales! I'm a Product Manager based in New York City. Born and raised in the sunny capital of the Philippines. A coding bootcamp and business school graduate. I've accomplished a total of 11 internships and with my ~5 years of experience, I worked on digital projects for Bed Bath & Beyond, J.Crew, Michael Kors, MTV News and Paramount Network.

My 9 to 5 involves leading scrum teams throughout the entire product or software development lifecycle. Often times being the team’s cheerleader. When I'm not on my laptop, you'll catch me in EDM shows, or reading business or self-help books, struggling with tennis or taking dance classes. Fun fact: I used to do competitive dancing, I’ve been on ~10 community dance teams and also back-up danced for Filipino popstars on their overseas shows/concerts! I love all things with good music, good energy and good vibrations. :)

What I Do

Product Management

As a PM, I aim to influence without authority. My day to day revolves around understanding the business objectives but focus on the execution of solutions. I collaborate with multiple stakeholders, prioritize the backlog, empathize with customers, gather requirements and write PRDs, oversee the sprint progress and build up the team spirit with a goal of having shared understanding.

Analytics & Strategy

While being customer-centric, I aspire to be a data-driven PM and my mission is to integrate data science and analytics thinking, product metrics, UX research, A/B test experiments, competitive and market analysis, and organization goals to drive priority of product initiatives.

Design & Development

As a former web developer, I can be an asset to non-technical teams as I have the ability to represent, speak and understand the language of designers, developers or engineers. My tech stack knowledge is evolving as I tackle multiple projects, engage in technical groomings and manage the collaboration between cross-functional teams.

Agile Project Management

With roadmap planning & execution, it’s my duty to support the development or engineering team and provide guidance on sprint goals and timelines. With agile as a way of thinking, requirements are always changing and it’s my responsibility to communicate clear project scope, minimize blockers, manage release documentation and optimize delivery.
